Why Layer 2 matters

Ethereum security, smoother everyday use.

Ethereum got crowded

Mainnet is secure, but high demand can make transactions slower and more expensive for regular users.

Arbitrum bundles activity

Arbitrum processes transactions off mainnet, groups them, and posts compressed proof back to Ethereum.

Lower cost, familiar tools

Users can interact with DeFi and apps using Ethereum-style wallets while paying much lower gas fees.

Real-world benefit

Small transactions become practical again.

On Ethereum mainnet, a tiny swap or game action can become expensive during busy periods. On Arbitrum, the same style of interaction is usually cheaper because many transactions share the cost of settlement.
